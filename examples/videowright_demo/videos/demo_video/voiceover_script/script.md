# Videowright Explainer

## cold-open
The video you're watching and this voice were generated by Videowright in Claude Code, with a single prompt.

## title-card
Videowright turns a coding agent into a video team, using any web technologies.

## web-tech-gallery
That includes animated SVG. Any library, like charting libraries. Advanced 3D and motion, like Three.js or Lottie. Even your real product UI, rendered from your app's codebase. If your stack runs in a browser, Videowright supports it.

## interactive-dev
Request changes in your coding agent, and the preview hot reloads.

## pixel-perfect-export
One command exports your video. It's deterministic, pixel-perfect rendering. Not a screen capture.

## voiceover-sync
Your script generates an AI narration. The pace of the narration drives the video timing. Edit a line and the video re-syncs.

## any-coding-agent
And Videowright works in every major coding agent.

## install-cta
Install Videowright and ask your agent to build your video. You'll have a video, before your coffee is cold.
